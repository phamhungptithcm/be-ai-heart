# Keep customer and owner flows separate

The platform has three surfaces on purpose: website for acquisition, portal for customer workspaces, and admin for internal control.

## Portal responsibilities

The portal should show synced repositories, document memory, diagram artifacts, benchmark proof, and usage signals that help a team decide whether expansion is justified.

## Admin responsibilities

The admin surface should show customer intake, support load, benchmark health, and revenue context without exposing internal controls to customers.

## Why this matters for trust

Smaller teams buy faster when the product feels understandable. Clear surface boundaries reduce fear that the platform is collecting more than it needs or hiding too much behind support.
