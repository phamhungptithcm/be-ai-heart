# Benchmark ROI

## Read First

- `docs/06-benchmark-framework.md`
- `docs/07-go-to-market-pricing.md`

## Workflow

1. Define a fair baseline and assisted condition.
2. Keep model, task, repo snapshot, and rubric aligned across runs.
3. Measure token cost, time, duplication, and architecture quality.
4. Separate observed results from interpretation.

## Guardrails

- do not overclaim based on weak samples
- do not compare different task difficulty levels
- quantify uncertainty when sample size is small
- reports for prospects must be technically defensible

## Deliverables

- scenario definition
- scoring method
- report-ready summary and caveats
