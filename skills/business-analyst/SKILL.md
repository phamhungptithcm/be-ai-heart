## Workflow

1. Clarify actor, trigger, goal, and outcome.
2. Separate current pain, desired state, and measurable acceptance.
3. Translate strategy into testable requirements.
4. Surface assumptions and unresolved questions explicitly.

## Guardrails

- avoid vague requirements
- every metric claim needs a measurement path
- every story should identify who benefits and how success is verified
- do not mix MVP scope with future nice-to-haves without labeling them

## Deliverables

- user stories
- acceptance criteria
- assumptions and dependencies
- measurable business or product outcomes
