## Problem

Selling tolling software is hard before implementation starts. Buyers want proof that the team understands roadside events, tag and plate matching, account funding, invoices, violations, disputes, fulfillment, reporting, and agency-specific policy.

## Solution

The Domain Demo Kit packages tolling domain memory into sales and discovery assets: polished website copy, UI prototype specs, architecture diagrams, DB and service drafts, safe demo data, demo scripts, RFP starter, and ROI hypotheses.

## Back-Office Modules

- Agent Account 360
- Customer search
- Funds and payments
- Trips, tolls, and invoices
- Violations, disputes, and cases
- Inventory and transponder fulfillment
- Notification center
- Reports dashboard

## Customer Portal

Customers can see account status, trips, invoices, vehicles, tags, disputes, cases, notification preferences, and official payment guidance. Risky actions are marked as requiring later auth, policy, and audit implementation.

## Security And Payments

The demo assumes hosted/tokenized payment capture, fake records, masked identifiers, permission-aware staff actions, and audit-first workflows for money, PII, evidence, notices, and disputes.

## Benchmark / ROI

The kit is designed to measure time to prototype, token savings, reduced duplicate work, fewer missed requirements, better docs/spec alignment, and support workflow completeness.
