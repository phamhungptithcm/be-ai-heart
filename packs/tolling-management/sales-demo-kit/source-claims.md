## Claim Review Rules

- Mark ROI as hypothesis until measured by a benchmark run.
- Mark toll rates, fees, notice periods, legal outcomes, and retention rules as agency-specific discovery.
- Do not imply public agencies endorse the demo.
- Do not use production PII, actual plates, payment account data, or credential material in examples.
