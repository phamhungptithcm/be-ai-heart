# Executive One-Pager

## Problem

Software teams selling tolling platforms need to look credible before they have a funded implementation. Tolling buyers expect more than a generic SaaS mockup. They want to see account management, tag and plate workflows, roadside events, trip posting, payments, invoices, violations, disputes, fulfillment, reporting, security, and agency-specific policy.

## Solution

The Tolling Sales Demo Kit turns the Tolling Management domain pack into a customer-ready package:

- Polished demo website
- Back-office and customer portal prototype surfaces
- Agent Account 360 demo
- Roadside-to-back-office workflow
- Architecture, service, API, DB, and ERD drafts
- Safe fake demo data
- Demo scripts
- Proposal/RFP starter
- Benchmark/ROI hypothesis
- Source-backed claims register

## Modules

| Module | Demo value |
|---|---|
| Agent Account 360 | Shows call-center readiness and safe action flow. |
| Customer portal | Shows self-service bill pay, trips, disputes, vehicles, and tags. |
| Payments/funds | Shows tokenized payment assumption, ledger, approvals, and audit. |
| Violations/disputes/cases | Shows support depth and policy variability. |
| Inventory/fulfillment | Shows transponder replacement and stock control. |
| Roadside/event flow | Shows tag/plate capture, image review, dedupe, rating, posting. |
| Reporting | Shows operational, finance, SLA, and support visibility. |
| Security/privacy | Shows PII, plate image, payment, audit, and scam-safety posture. |

## Value

- Faster sales discovery
- Better customer conversations
- Stronger screenshots and demo calls
- Less repeated domain explanation
- Clearer implementation scope
- Better source discipline for claims
- Safer demo data and payment posture

## Rollout Phases

1. Static demo kit and microsite.
2. Agency-specific overlay for buyer discovery.
3. Clickable prototype with fake data.
4. Runtime MVP for selected workflows.
5. Pilot benchmark comparing baseline vs pack-assisted implementation.

## Security Posture

The demo uses fake data only. Payment flows assume hosted/tokenized payment capture. Staff actions that affect money, PII, evidence, or disputes are modeled with reason codes, permissions, and audit. Scam-safe messaging is part of the notification and support story.

## ROI Hypothesis

The kit is designed to measure reductions in sales-prep time, time to prototype, repeated prompting, missed requirements, and support workflow gaps. No savings are claimed as proven until observed benchmark evidence exists.
