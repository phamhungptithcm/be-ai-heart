# Investor One-Pager

## Company

`be-ai-heart`

## Category

Context infrastructure for AI-assisted software engineering

## Thesis

AI coding adoption is growing faster than organizational control. Teams are spending money on tokens and cleanup because models do not retain durable project understanding. `be-ai-heart` builds the persistent codebase memory layer that makes AI coding cheaper, safer, and more architecture-aware.

## Problem

- AI loses project context
- Teams repeatedly pay to re-explain the codebase
- Duplicate implementations increase
- Review overhead stays high
- Enterprises lack governance and ROI clarity

## Product

`be-ai-heart` scans repositories, builds a semantic graph of code, decisions, and policies, then serves task-specific context packs to AI tools through CLI and MCP.

- Benchmark-led ROI proof
- Strong enterprise narrative: cost control plus AI safety plus architecture consistency
